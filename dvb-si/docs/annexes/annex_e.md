# Annex E — Usage of the Scrambling Descriptor

**Spec:** ETSI EN 300 468 v1.19.1 Annex E (normative)

Defines how the `scrambling_descriptor` (tag 0x65) shall be used to signal
that a component is scrambled. Required in the PMT ES_info loop for any scrambled elementary stream.
